# Flock — Church Management, Simplified

> Flock is a modern, cloud-based church management platform built for
> congregations of all sizes. Manage your people, giving, events, and
> facilities from one beautifully simple place.

---

## What is Flock?

Running a church involves a lot of moving parts — tracking members,
coordinating volunteers, managing donations, booking rooms, and keeping
everyone connected. Most churches juggle spreadsheets, paper lists, and
a handful of disconnected tools to get it all done.

Flock brings everything together in one platform, purpose-built for how
churches actually work. Whether you're a small congregation of 50 or a
multi-campus church of thousands, Flock scales with you.

---

## Core Features

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Member Management
Your congregation is at the heart of everything. Flock gives you a
complete, searchable directory of every person connected to your church.

- Full member profiles with contact details and photos
- Family and household grouping
- Member status tracking (visitor, active, inactive)
- Pastoral notes and care history
- CSV import and export
- Powerful search and filtering

---

### 💛 Giving & Donations
Flock makes generosity easy — for your members and your finance team.
All giving flows directly to your church's own bank account. Flock never
holds your funds.

- Online giving via credit/debit card and ACH bank transfer
- One-time and recurring giving schedules
- Donor-covers-fees option
- Manual cash and check entry for Sunday offerings
- Batch offering entry and reconciliation
- Designated giving funds (General, Missions, Building Fund, and more)
- Tax-compliant contribution statements — calendar year and fiscal year
- Giving dashboards and fund summaries
- Secure by design — card data never touches Flock's servers

---

### 📅 Events & Attendance
From Sunday services to outreach events, Flock keeps your calendar
organised and your attendance records accurate.

- Single and recurring event creation
- Custom event categories
- Event registration and RSVP management
- Attendance recording — headcount or individual member check-in
- Attendance history per member and per event
- Multi-campus event support

---

### 👥 Groups & Ministries
Every church runs on its groups. Flock makes it easy to manage every
ministry team, small group, and committee under one roof.

- Unlimited groups with custom types (small group, ministry team,
  committee, choir, and more)
- Member enrollment with leader and co-leader roles
- Sub-groups for groups that split and meet independently
  (e.g. a Married Couples group with Men's and Women's sub-groups)
- Group attendance tracking
- Group-level communication

---

### 🏛️ Room Booking & Facility Management
Stop the double-booking chaos. Flock gives you a clear picture of every
space in your building and who has it when.

- Room and space inventory per campus
- Equipment and resource tracking per room
- Booking requests with conflict detection
- Per-room approval workflows
- Bookings linked to events automatically
- Campus-wide availability calendar

---

### 🗓️ Scheduling
Coordinating who serves where on Sunday shouldn't take hours. Flock
makes service and event scheduling fast, clear, and conflict-free.

- Service and event schedule builder
- Custom role slots (Worship Leader, Greeter, Sound Tech, Usher,
  and any role your church uses)
- Assign members to slots with automated email notifications
- Confirm or decline via a simple email link — no login required
- Conflict detection — no one gets double-booked
- Member availability and blackout date management
- Schedule publishing

---

### 📊 Reporting & Analytics
The numbers that matter, when you need them. Flock surfaces the insights
your leadership team needs to make informed decisions.

- Attendance trends over time
- Giving summaries by fund, period, and method
- Member growth tracking
- Group attendance reports
- Schedule coverage reports
- Customisable dashboard for every role
- Export any report to CSV
- Scheduled report delivery by email

---

### 🏢 Multi-Campus Support
Managing more than one location? Flock treats every campus as a
first-class part of your organisation — not an afterthought.

- Unlimited campuses per church
- Members, events, rooms, and groups scoped per campus
- Cross-campus reporting and admin visibility
- Campus-specific dashboards

---

### 🔒 Roles & Permissions
Not everyone needs access to everything. Flock's role system keeps
sensitive data safe while giving your team the access they need.

- Church Admin — full access to all modules and settings
- Staff — operational access to members, events, scheduling, and rooms
- Volunteer — view their own schedule and record attendance
- Custom role assignment per user

---

### 📧 Notifications & Communication
Flock keeps your team informed automatically — no manual chasing
required.

- Automated email notifications for schedule assignments
- Giving receipts sent instantly on donation
- Room booking confirmations and approval notifications
- User invitation emails for onboarding new staff
- Password reset and account management emails

## Built for Security & Privacy

Flock is designed with your congregation's data and your church's
finances protected at every layer.

- **PCI Compliant giving** — card and bank data never touches Flock's
  servers. All payments are processed through Stripe's secure
  infrastructure.
- **Direct deposit** — giving goes straight to your church's bank
  account. Flock never holds your funds.
- **Role-based access** — your team only sees what they need to see.
- **Data isolation** — every church's data is fully separated. Your
  information is never shared with or visible to other organisations.
- **Encrypted in transit** — all data is transmitted over HTTPS.
