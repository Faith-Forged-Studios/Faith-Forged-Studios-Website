# Projects

Faith Forged Studios builds software that makes a real difference — from faith-centered applications to tools that solve everyday business challenges. Here's a look at what we're working on.

## Current Projects

### [The SOAP Dish](/projects/the-soap-dish)
`🟢 Active Development`

The SOAP Dish is a Bible study application at its core. This was the first project we started, and since it was originally released in 2022 it has been completely revamped and is currently in the process of yet another full rewrite with tons of new features. For a full breakdown of The SOAP Dish features, visit the project's main page.

### [Flock](/projects/flock)
`🔵 Early Development`

Flock is in the early stages of its development lifecycle. This idea started after talking with staff members at various churches where all of their day-to-day operations were being managed across multiple platforms. Meetings were scheduled in Outlook, rooms were booked in another application, and event registration lived in yet another system. Flock aims to bring it all under one roof.

## Coming Soon

### Basalt
`⚪ Planning`

Basalt is a self-hosted cloud administration and compliance platform built for Microsoft 365 tenant management. Designed for MSPs, managed IT teams, and internal IT departments, Basalt takes an automation-first, compliance-driven approach to multi-tenant management. Development begins after Flock is complete.

### Vigil
`⚪ Planning`

Vigil is a SaaS platform designed for small-to-mid security guard companies that are underserved by expensive enterprise solutions. From scheduling and dispatch to incident reporting and client portals, Vigil aims to be an affordable, all-in-one tool that keeps operations simple and teams connected.
